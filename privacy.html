Privacy Policy

Three Lanterns Studios · Lexitris

Overview

Three Lanterns Studios ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Lexitris mobile game and web app. This policy explains what data is collected when you play Lexitris and how it is used.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not personally collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. All data collection described below is handled by trusted third-party services.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, precise location, or any personally identifiable information directly. We do not operate our own analytics or tracking systems. Your game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

Leaderboard & Score Submission

Lexitris includes an optional global leaderboard powered by Google Firebase Firestore. If you choose to submit a score, the following data is stored:

  • Your chosen display name (auto-generated or entered by you, up to 16 characters)
  • Your score and game mode
  • The date of submission

This data is stored in Google Firebase and is publicly visible on the leaderboard. No account or sign-in is required. Do not enter real personal information as your display name. Firebase data handling is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Lexitris displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalised ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and general location
  • App usage and interaction data

This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time by visiting google.com/settings/ads or through your device's advertising settings.

Ads can be permanently removed via the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase.

In-App Purchases

Lexitris offers an optional "Remove Ads" purchase processed through Google Play Billing. We do not handle, store, or have access to any payment information. All transactions are managed entirely by Google Play and are subject to Google Play's Terms of Service.

Local Storage

Lexitris stores your game progress, high scores, settings, and preferences locally on your device using browser local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

Lexitris is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information through our leaderboard, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

Data Retention & Deletion

Local game data can be cleared at any time by clearing your app or browser data. Leaderboard entries submitted to Firebase are retained indefinitely but contain no personally identifiable information. To request removal of a leaderboard entry, contact us with the display name and score.

Third-Party Services

Lexitris uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising
  • Google Firebase Firestore — Leaderboard data
  • Google Play Billing — In-app purchases

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Lexitris after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact Three Lanterns Studios via the contact information provided on our Google Play Store listing.
