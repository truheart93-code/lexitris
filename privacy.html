Privacy Policy

Three Lanterns Studios · Lexitris

Overview

Three Lanterns Studios ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Lexitris mobile game. This policy explains what data is collected when you play Lexitris and how it is used.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not personally collect, store, or share any data ourselves. All data collection is handled by third-party services described below.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information. We do not run our own analytics or tracking systems. Your game progress and scores are stored locally on your device only.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Lexitris displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalised ads, including device identifiers, IP address, and usage data. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting: google.com/settings/ads

For more information on how Google uses data: policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

Lexitris offers an optional "Remove Ads" purchase processed through Google Play Billing. We do not handle or store any payment information. All transactions are managed by Google Play and subject to Google's Terms of Service.

Local Storage

Lexitris stores your game progress, high scores, and settings locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

Lexitris is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Lexitris after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Three Lanterns Studios via the contact information provided on our Google Play Store listing.
